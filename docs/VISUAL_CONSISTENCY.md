# Visual consistency (education)

Public summary — dark-first iSystem PWAs. Full operator standards stay in the private monorepo.

---

## Principles

- **Dark-first** — `color-scheme: dark` on `html`; no white browser sliders or `<select>` popups
- **Global tokens** — shared palette from [isystem.app](https://isystem.app) theme layer
- **Touch** — 44px minimum tap targets; safe-area insets on iPhone notch
- **Responsive** — test 390 portrait/landscape, 768 tablet, 1400 desktop
- **TIP** — optional PayPal support link in nav or footer (kindness, not nagging)

---

## Chrome every product shares

- Nav link to [isystem.app](https://isystem.app)
- Footer: Privacy · Terms · Cookies · Support · license · official URL
- PWA install affordance where applicable
