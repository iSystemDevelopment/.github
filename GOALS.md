## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Quality Metrics
- Code coverage > 80%
- Documentation coverage: 100% of public APIs
- Zero critical security vulnerabilities
- Average build time < 5 minutes

### Productivity Metrics
- Sprint completion rate > 85%
- Deployment frequency: Daily or on-demand
- Mean time to recovery (MTTR) < 1 hour
- Change failure rate < 5%

### Collaboration Metrics
- AI-assisted commits > 70%
- Code review response time < 24 hours
- Documentation update frequency: Weekly
- Team satisfaction score > 4/5

## Success Criteria

A goal is considered achieved when:

1. **Completion**: All tasks under the goal are finished
2. **Quality**: Results meet our quality standards
3. **Documentation**: Changes are fully documented
4. **Validation**: Success is measurable and verified
5. **Sustainability**: Solution is maintainable long-term

## Review & Updates

Goals are reviewed and updated:
- **Weekly**: Progress check on short-term goals
- **Monthly**: Review and adjust medium-term goals
- **Quarterly**: Assess long-term goals and strategy
- **Annually**: Major strategic planning and goal setting
